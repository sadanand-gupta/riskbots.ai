Tectalik RiskBots Cloud Application

Tectalik RiskBots is a cutting-edge cloud application designed to provide continuous risk monitoring across key financial and operational processes. Built with 20 years of risk management and consulting expertise, it proactively identifies revenue leakages, frauds, and errors in critical business functions, ensuring enhanced governance and compliance.

Comprehensive Coverage Across Business Functions


Tectalik RiskBots automates risk monitoring across:

Purchase to Pay

Detects fraudulent vendors, duplicate payments, and non-compliant procurement practices.

Order to Collection

Identifies irregularities in revenue recognition, customer credit risks, and delayed collections.

Finance & Accounts

Ensures accuracy in financial reporting, reconciliations, and regulatory compliance.

Payroll

Flags ghost employees, unauthorized salary payments, and anomalies in payroll disbursement.

Statutory Compliances

Monitors adherence to legal and regulatory requirements, reducing penalties and legal risks.

Delegation of Authority

Tracks deviations from approval hierarchies to prevent unauthorized transactions.

Segregation of Duties (SoD) Exposures

Identifies potential conflicts in user access and role assignments within enterprise systems.

Empowering Internal Auditors & Senior Management

Tectalik RiskBots is an invaluable tool for Internal Auditors, significantly reducing their effort in data analysis and risk identification. For Senior Management, it provides real-time assurance and confidence by continuously tracking and reporting financial and operational risks.

Seamless Integration with Leading ERPs

RiskBots seamlessly integrates with SAP, Tally, and other financial ERPs, both on-cloud and on-premise, ensuring smooth implementation without disrupting existing workflows.
